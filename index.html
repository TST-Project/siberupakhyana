King Śibi in the Vahnipurāṇa

A critical edition & translation of the śiber upākhyāna

Charles Li

Last updated: 16 Decemmber 2022.

This digital edition of the Vahnipurāṇa śiber upākhyāna forms the suppelementary material to the article King Śibi in the Vahnipurāṇa. Apart from the critical text, diplomatic transcriptions of each witness are also included.
